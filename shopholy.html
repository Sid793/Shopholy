Our story

We make objects
you'll keep forever

Sopholy was born from a simple belief — that the things around us should be beautiful, meaningful, and honestly made.

How it began
"We were tired of beautiful things that broke. So we decided to make our own."

In 2013, founders Élise Moreau and Julien Vautier set up a small studio in the 11th arrondissement of Paris with a straightforward mission: create everyday objects that outlast trends.

What started as a curated selection of 12 handmade pieces has grown into a globally loved collection of over 250 products — each one still meeting the same exacting standards as the very first.

What guides us

Our principles

01
Radical honesty
We tell you exactly how and where each product is made. No greenwashing, no vague claims — only transparent, verifiable information.
02
Enduring quality
We hold every product to a lifetime standard. If it can't be repaired or isn't built to last, it doesn't make our collection.
03
Fair partnerships
Our makers are paid fairly and treated as true collaborators. We build long-term relationships, not extractive ones.
04
Minimal footprint
We choose organic, recycled, or regenerative materials wherever possible and offset what we can't yet avoid.
05
Considered design
Every Sopholy piece is designed to be genuinely useful and quietly beautiful — never decorative for its own sake.
06
Community first
We give back 1% of all revenue to artisan communities and cultural preservation programs worldwide.

Common questions

Frequently Asked

What is your shipping policy? +
We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $80. Orders typically ship within 1–2 business days. Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days, while express options (2–3 days) are available at checkout.
Can I return a product? +
Yes. We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Simply contact us at returns@sopholy.com and we'll arrange a collection.
Are your products really sustainable? +
Absolutely. Every product page includes a full materials breakdown and supply chain transparency report. We use certified organic, recycled, or responsibly sourced materials exclusively, and publish an annual Impact Report.
Do you offer wholesale or trade pricing? +
Yes, we work with interior designers, hotels, and retailers. Please contact our trade team at trade@sopholy.com with your business details and we'll be in touch within 48 hours.
How do I care for my Sopholy pieces? +
Each product comes with a detailed care card. General guidance: avoid harsh chemicals, store in a cool dry place, and handle with care. Most of our products can also be repaired — contact us and we'll advise.
